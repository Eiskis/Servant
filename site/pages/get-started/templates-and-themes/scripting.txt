
For more advanced use cases, PHP templates are supported. They allow you to write dynamic pages, where you can generate content based what's available on your site or environment.



## Examples

#### Dynamic details of current page

	// Dynamic title
	$parents = $servant->page()->parents();
	echo '<h1>'.(!empty($parents) ? $parents : $servant->site()->name()).': '.$servant->page()->name().'</h1>'
